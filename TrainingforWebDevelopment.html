Keys to becoming a Web Debeloper

There are four paths most people take to become web developers:

  • Instructure-led Training
  • Self Study
  • Working on Projects
  • Learning that only happens as a result of working with real clients

Instructure-led means learning from an instructure who follows a curriculum with exercises, lesson plans, and other tools.This type of learning happens at a school, in a boot camp, or in a workshop or conference. This works best for someone with less experience who wants to be able to ask questions and work with others in this industry and software changes so rapidly so it’s not as important to learn specific skills, but rather learn the basics, and then adjust quickly to new techs. Some of the keys to becoming a great developer are to learn to read online documents, examine other developers’ code, and explore new ideas. No amount of reading books or watching videos will make you into a great programmer. Some learning is only going to happen as a result of making mistakes with real things, making mistakes actually makes you a stronger developer in proving ur ability to find, test, and anticipate errors.

Friendly Advice

As many Developers study for this field they will notice that practice makes perfect and mistakes are good to make when beginning a project and also getting involved with others project will give you connection in the Web community and will learn from others about the different aspects of Web Development.p>
